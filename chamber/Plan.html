EcoTravel Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

EcoTravel Hub — This name was selected because the site focuses on sustainable travel tips, eco-friendly destinations, and resources for travelers who want to minimize their environmental impact.

Optional domain: ecotravelhub.org

Site Purpose

The site provides guides, articles, and resources about eco-friendly travel. It will feature destination highlights, sustainable travel gear recommendations, and a community forum for sharing experiences.

Scenarios

  • “What are the top eco-friendly destinations in Africa?”
  • “Which travel gear is best for minimizing waste while on the road?”

Color Scheme

Primary color: Deep Blue (#006699) — used for headings and borders.

Accent color: Orange (#cc6600) — used for highlights and call-to-action elements.

Background: Light Gray (#f5f5f5).

Typography

Headings: Georgia, serif (for a professional, elegant look).

Body text: Arial, sans-serif (for readability and clarity).
