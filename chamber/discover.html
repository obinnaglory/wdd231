Discover Imo State

History

Imo State was created in 1976 from the former East Central State. Known for its role in the Nigerian Civil War and as the birthplace of many prominent Nigerians, Imo is rich in Igbo culture and history.

Demographics

Population: ~5.4 million | Ethnic groups: Igbo | Languages: English, Igbo | Major cities: Owerri, Orlu, Okigwe
